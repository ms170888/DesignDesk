Pricing Breakdown

DesignDesk starts at £29/mo for solo designers, £79/mo for studios (3 users), and £149/mo for enterprise. Annual billing saves 20%.

Houzz Pro starts at $149/mo for the Essential plan. Additional users cost $60/user/mo. The Pro plan is $249/mo. That's $209+/month for a studio of 3.

For a 3-person studio, DesignDesk costs £79/mo vs $249+/mo for Houzz Pro — saving you over £1,500/year.

When to Choose Houzz Pro

Houzz Pro is the better choice if you're a US-based firm that relies on the Houzz marketplace for lead generation. Their 50M+ monthly visitor marketplace is unmatched for getting new clients. If lead gen is your primary need, Houzz Pro pays for itself.

When to Choose DesignDesk

DesignDesk is the better choice if you need an all-in-one platform that combines design tools with business management. Especially if you're UK-based (FreeAgent + UK suppliers), want mood boards, need dual invoicing, or want the best value per feature.

Our Verdict

For most interior design studios, DesignDesk offers significantly more value. You get mood boards, dual invoicing, accounting integration, and an AI assistant — all at a fraction of Houzz Pro's cost. Houzz Pro only wins if you need their marketplace for lead generation.
