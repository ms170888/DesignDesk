Pricing

DesignDesk: £29/mo (1 user), £79/mo (3 users), £149/mo (unlimited). Flat plan pricing.

Mydoma: $64-84/user/mo. 3D visualiser is an extra $30/mo add-on. A 3-person studio on Essentials: $216/mo + $30 visualiser = $246/month.

Where Mydoma Wins

Mydoma has more mature client communication features — built-in messaging, design packages with itemised selection sheets, and contract templates. If client communication is your #1 priority and you don't need scheduling or floor plans, Mydoma is solid.

Where DesignDesk Wins

DesignDesk wins on features per pound: Gantt scheduling, floor plans, AI assistant, dual invoicing, and UK accounting integration are all included. Mydoma charges extra for 3D and doesn't offer scheduling, floor plans, or AI at any price.

Our Verdict

DesignDesk is the clear winner for studios that need more than just client management. You get everything Mydoma offers (mood boards, client portal, invoicing) plus scheduling, floor plans, AI, and accounting integration — all at a lower price.
