Detailed Reviews

1. DesignDesk — Best All-in-One Platform

Price: £29-149/mo Best for: Solo to mid-size studios Free trial: Yes, no credit card

DesignDesk is the only platform that combines professional design tools (mood boards, floor plans, AI assistant) with full business management (procurement, Gantt scheduling, dual invoicing) and accounting integration (FreeAgent, Xero, QuickBooks). Used by designers in 30+ countries.

Pros

  • All-in-one: design + business + financials
  • Only tool with FreeAgent integration
  • Preloaded regional supplier databases
  • AI-powered design assistant
  • Beautiful branded client portal
  • Most affordable per-feature

Cons

  • Newer to market (launched 2026)
  • Supplier database growing globally
  • Mobile app coming soon

2. Houzz Pro — Best for Lead Generation

Price: $149-249/mo + $60/extra user Best for: US-based firms wanting leads Free trial: 30 days

Houzz Pro is the most feature-rich option and benefits from Houzz's massive homeowner marketplace. Strong AI features (AutoMate) for estimates and takeoffs. However, it's expensive, US-centric, lacks mood boards, and has no Xero/FreeAgent integration.

Pros

  • Built-in lead generation from Houzz marketplace
  • Strong AI (AutoMate) for estimates
  • 3D floor plans
  • Good mobile app

Cons

  • Very expensive ($149+/mo)
  • No mood board tool
  • No Xero or FreeAgent integration
  • US-focused, limited UK supplier data

3. Programa — Best for FF&E Specification

Price: $59/user/mo ($47 annual) Best for: Firms focused on product specification Free trial: 7 days

Programa excels at FF&E schedules and product specification with its Chrome clipper. Good Kanban project management and branded presentations. Lacks floor plans, has limited AI, no Gantt charts, and has a steep learning curve.

Pros

  • Excellent product clipper (Chrome extension)
  • Strong FF&E specification workflow
  • Good client approval features
  • Stripe integration for client payments

Cons

  • No floor plans or 3D rendering
  • No Gantt chart scheduling
  • Steep learning curve
  • No mobile app
  • No Xero/FreeAgent integration

4. Mydoma Studio — Best Client Communication

Price: $64-84/user/mo Best for: Firms prioritising client experience Free trial: 15 days

Mydoma shines at client communication with built-in messaging, design packages, and contract management. Has Stripe + QuickBooks integration. Mood boards are good but no floor plans, no scheduling, and the UI feels dated.

Pros

  • Best client messaging portal
  • Design packages and contracts
  • Stripe + QuickBooks integration
  • Mood boards included

Cons

  • No scheduling or Gantt charts
  • No floor plans
  • No AI features
  • UI feels outdated
  • No Xero/FreeAgent integration

5. DesignFiles — Best Budget Option

Price: $49-69/mo (unlimited projects) Best for: Solo designers on a budget Free trial: Available

DesignFiles offers the best value for solo designers with unlimited projects, mood boards, 3D floor plans, AI rendering, and a Chrome product clipper. QuickBooks integration on the Full Service plan. No scheduling and limited team features.

Pros

  • Affordable with unlimited projects
  • AI rendering for mood boards
  • 3D floor plans
  • Chrome product clipper

Cons

  • Limited scheduling features
  • No team collaboration (1 user focus)
  • No Xero/FreeAgent integration
  • QuickBooks only on higher plan
