## 1.1 HOUZZ PRO (houzz.co.uk/pro)
**Status:** Absorbed Ivy (acquired 2018-2019). Ivy no longer accepts new users; all features migrated to Houzz Pro.

### Pricing (UK)
| Tier | Monthly (USD) | Annual (USD) | UK (GBP est.) |
|------|--------------|--------------|----------------|
| Essential (Designers) | $149/mo | $99/mo billed annually | ~£54-£120/mo |
| Pro (Contractors) | $249/mo | $159/mo billed annually | ~£130-£200/mo |
| Custom | Contact sales | Contact sales | £350+/mo reported |

*Note: UK GBP pricing varies; Capterra UK lists from £54/mo, some users report £4,200+/year.*

### Features by Category

**Project Management**
- Project dashboard & scheduling
- Task management with milestones
- Daily logs (progress notes, photos, files by project)
- Selections management (by room/phase/category)
- Bid management
- Procurement tracking
- Time tracking

**Financial/Invoicing**
- Estimates & proposals (customisable templates)
- Invoicing & online payments
- Change orders (Custom tier)
- Budget management (Custom tier)
- QuickBooks integration

**Design Tools**
- 3D Floor Plans (drag-and-drop)
- Mood boards / selections boards
- Product clipper (browser extension)
- Home Depot / custom catalogue integration
- Takeoffs from floor plans
- 3D room scanning (mobile)

**Client Management**
- Full CRM with lead tracking
- Customisable lead stages and tags
- Client portal (view schedules, selections, invoices)
- Email marketing campaigns
- Lead generation from Houzz marketplace
- AutoMate AI insights for leads

**Team Collaboration**
- Multi-user access
- Role-based permissions
- Daily logs shared with team/clients

**Marketing**
- Custom website builder
- SEO profile on Houzz marketplace
- Email campaign tools
- Review management

**Integrations**
- QuickBooks
- Home Depot catalogue
- Houzz product marketplace

**AI Features**
- AutoMate AI (lead insights, automated follow-ups)
- AI-powered project suggestions

**Mobile App:** Yes (iOS & Android) — manage projects, leads, finances, 3D scans

## 1.8 IVY (ivy.co — Now Part of Houzz Pro)
**Status: DISCONTINUED as standalone product.** Ivy was acquired by Houzz in 2018-2019 and merged into Houzz Pro. No new Ivy memberships. Existing users are being migrated to Houzz Pro with a 1-year pricing match.

**What designers loved about Ivy (and miss):**
- Simple, intuitive interface designed specifically for interior designers
- Clean invoicing and procurement
- Industry-specific workflows
- Less bloated than Houzz Pro

**What changed:** Houzz Pro is broader (construction + design), less design-specific. Many designers report dissatisfaction with the transition.
